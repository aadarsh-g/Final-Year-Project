# Images Directory

## Structure

Place your images in the following folders:

### `/images/auth/`
- `login-illustration.png` - Illustration for login page
- `signup-illustration.png` - Illustration for signup page

### `/images/hero/`
- `hero-reading.jpg` or `hero-reading.png` - Boy reading book image for hero section
- `welcome-books.jpg` or `welcome-books.png` - Books image for welcome section

### `/images/books/`
- `it-ends-with-us.jpg` - Book cover for "It Ends With Us"
- `great-gatsby.jpg` - Book cover for "The Great Gatsby"
- `fault-in-our-stars.jpg` - Book cover for "The Fault In Our Stars"
- `harry-potter.jpg` - Book cover for "Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone"

### `/images/logo/`
- `logo.png` or `logo.svg` - Your Bookify logo (optional)

## How to Add Images

1. Download or place your images in the appropriate folders above
2. The landing page will automatically use these images
3. Supported formats: `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png`, `.webp`, `.svg`

## Image Recommendations

- **Hero images**: 1200x800px or larger
- **Book covers**: 400x600px (2:3 aspect ratio)
- **Logo**: 200x200px or SVG
- Use optimized images (compressed) for better performance
